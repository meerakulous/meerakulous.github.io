About Me

I am a sixth year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Michigan. My research focuses on developing accurate machine learning models given resource constraints (i.e. limited memory; and limited time).

I am advised by Professor Jenna Wiens. Part of my work is funded by a Graduate Fellowship for STEM Diversity.

I received my bachelor's degree from Caltech (major: EE, minor: CS).

In the past, I have developed computational tools for multiple domains, including autonomous systems control, astronomy, and population studies.

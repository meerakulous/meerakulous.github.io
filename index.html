About Me

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Michigan. I work with Professor Jenna Wiens on developing novel machine learning techniques to classify healthcare data. Part of my work is funded by a Graduate Fellowship for STEM Diversity.

I received my bachelor's degree from Caltech (major: EE, minor: CS).

My previous experience includes

  • machine learning/autonomous systems control research at Caltech's CAST (Center for Autonomous Systems and Technology) with Professor Yisong Yue
  • software development and social network analysis at Rocketship.vc
  • spectral analysis of images of Jupiter at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory with Dr. Glenn Orton
  • mathematical modeling of populations facing extinction at Stanford with Professor Shripad Tuljapurkar
